  • • Objective : The Chief Minister Fellowship Program offers the youth an opportunity to be a part of the government.

  • • Provider : Govt. of Maharashtra.

  • • What for : This scholarship scheme is for the students studying in top hundred colleges across India such as IITs and IIMs.

  • • How many seats : N/A

    > Nature of assistance

  • • Things covered : The total amount of scholarship available over the duration of the degree programme, will be up to Rs/INR 2 Lakhs.

  • • Scholarship amount : Any Graduate 75,000 per month.

  • • Duration : 01 Year.

  • • Apply Link : mahades.maharashtra.gov.in

    > Qualifications

  • • Ranking requirement : N/A

  • • Academic qualification : The candidate should be a graduate from any discipline with minimum 60% marks and at least one year work experience. Visit official website link is given above.

  • • Income criteria : N/A

  • • Residential requirement : Yes you hold Indian citizenship.

  • • Any other (PAN/Bonds etc.): Proof of Citizenship (birth certificate), degree certificates, etc.

    > Schedule

  • • Advertisement (prospective dates/month): February.

  • • Application procedure : Visit website.

  • • Rounds : Two round (online test, essays and interview).

  • • How to prepare the best application: N/A
