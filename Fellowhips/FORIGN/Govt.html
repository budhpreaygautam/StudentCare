Government Fellowship : Abroad

1. National Overseas Scholarship

  • • Objective : The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment launched the National Overseas Scholarship to promote social and economic justice. This scholarship facilitates low-income students from Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Landless Agricultural Labourers, and Traditional Artisans to pursue Master's Degrees, Ph.D., and Post-Doctoral courses abroad.

  • • Provider : Government.

  • • What for : This scholarship facilitates low-income students from Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Landless Agricultural Labourers, and Traditional Artisans to pursue Master's Degrees, Ph.D., and Post-Doctoral courses abroad.

  • • How many seats : 30% of seats are reserved for female candidates.

    > Nature of assistance

  • • Things covered : Tuition fees, visa fees, book costs, and living expenses also medical insurance premium, poll tax, Maintenance fee, Contingency allowance, Air tickets and incidental journey allowance.

  • • Scholarship amount : N/A

  • • Duration : N/A

  • • Apply Link : National Overseas scholarship

    > Qualifications

  • • Ranking requirement : N/A

  • • Academic qualification : The candidate must have scored a minimum of 60% in the qualifying examination. The Bachelor's degree is the qualifying examination for the Master's Degree course, and Master's Degree is the qualifying examination for the Ph.D. courses.

  • • Income criteria : Family income should be less than INR 8,00,000 in the previous financial year.

  • • Residential requirement : Yes

  • • Any other (PAN/Bonds etc.): N/A

    > Schedule

  • • Advertisement (prospective dates/month): March.

  • • Application procedure :

    • o Go to the official scholarship portal of the Indian government, link is given above
    • o Register and login.
    • o Access the online form and fill out the application
    • o Documents required are attested copies of testimonials, study program details, photographs, etc.

  • • Rounds : N/A

  • • How to prepare the best application: N/A

  • • Things to keep in mind: The candidate should not be more than 35 years old and the scholarship can be awarded to a maximum of two members of the family. NOS is a central sector scholarship scheme to facilitate low incomes students belonging to the Scheduled Castes, Denotifie Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Tribes, Landless Agricultural Labourers and Traditional Artisans categories to obtain higher education abroad.

2. Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree

  • • Objective : Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degrees are Masters's level study programs, EMJM study program is designed and delivered by an international partnership of higher education institutions (HEIs).

  • • Provider : Erasmus Mundus.

  • • What for : Higher Education.

  • • How many seats : Variable

    > Nature of assistance

  • • Things covered : Tuition fees, traveling allowance, Round-trip air travel, insurance premium, and living expenses, job visa after completing Master’s/PhD.

  • • Scholarship amount : Monthly stipend of u to 1500 Euros.

  • • Duration : 6 academic years, covering at least 4 editions of the master’s programme.

  • • Apply Link : erasmus-mundus-catalogue_en

    > Qualifications

  • • Ranking requirement : N/A

  • • Academic qualification : The candidate should have a bachelor's degree and relevant work experience.

  • • Income criteria : Yes

  • • Residential requirement : The candidate must be an Indian national.

  • • Any other (PAN/Bonds etc.): N/A

    > Schedule

  • • Advertisement (prospective dates/month): November to December.

  • • Application procedure :

    • o Go to the official scholarship portal of the Indian government, link is given above
    • o Register and login.
    • o Access the online form and fill out the application
    • o Documents required are attested copies of testimonials, study program details, photographs, etc.

  • • Rounds : Interview round.

  • • How to prepare the best application: N/A

  • • Things to keep in mind: The candidate should not have resided in any European country for more than a year in the last five years and should not have been awarded a similar scholarship under the Erasmus Mundus Action 2 scheme.

3. Agatha Harrison Memorial Fellowship

  • • Objective : Ms. Agatha Harrison Memorial Fellowship is a fully funded Government scholarship for one candidate to study at St. Antony College, Oxford University London. The fellowship is exclusively administered by the HRD Ministry and is primarily targeted at students interested in pursuing an academic career in History, Political Science, and Economics.

  • • Provider : The Govt. of India.

  • • What for : Higher Education.

  • • How many seats : N/A

  • • Scholarship amount : INR 27 LAKHS.

  • • Duration : N/A

  • • Apply Link : proposal.sakshat.ac.in

    > Qualifications

  • • Ranking requirement : N/A

  • • Academic qualification :

    • o The candidate should have scored a minimum of 60% marks at the Master's degree level.
    • o A PhD Degree with a specialization in History, Political Science, or Economics.
    • o The candidate must have a specialization in history, economics, or political science.
    • o The candidate should have at least three years of teaching experience at the Graduate or Postgraduate level.
    • o The candidate should have a Ph.D. degree in the subject field or published works of equal merit.

  • • Income criteria : Yes

  • • Residential requirement : The candidate must be a permanent citizen of India.

  • • Any other (PAN/Bonds etc.): N/A

    > Schedule

  • • Advertisement (prospective dates/month): January.

  • • Application procedure :

    • o Go to the official scholarship portal of the Indian government, link is given above
    • o Register and login.
    • o Access the online form and fill out the application
    • o Documents required are attested copies of testimonials, study program details, photographs, etc.

  • • Rounds : Document verification and interview & Merit based.

  • • How to prepare the best application: N/A

4. Fulbright-Nehru Master’s Fellowship

  • • Objective : The Fulbright-Nehru Master’s Fellowship is a merit and need-based Indian government scholarship to provide outstanding Indian students an opportunity to pursue a master’s degree program at selected colleges and universities in the U.S.

  • • Provider : Trust.

  • • What for : fellowship for students who have leadership qualities, have done the U.S equivalent bachelor’s degree, and have a minimum of three years of professional work experience.

  • • How many seats : Variable.

    > Nature of assistance

  • • Things covered : Funding for tuition fees, living, and other associated expenses, J-1 visa support, Round-trip economy class air travel from the candidate's home city to the U.S., Accident and sickness coverage as per the guidelines of the U.S. government.

  • • Scholarship amount : N/A

  • • Duration : N/A

  • • Apply Link : apply.iie.org

    > Qualifications

  • • Ranking requirement : N/A

  • • Academic qualification : The candidate must have a U.S. equivalent bachelor's degree from an acknowledged Indian university with a minimum of 55%mark. The candidates must have a minimum of three year's full-time (paid) professional work experience relevant to the specified field of study. The candidate should have leadership qualities and community service experience.

  • • Income criteria : Yes

  • • Residential requirement : yes (The candidate should be an Indian citizen)

  • • Any other (PAN/Bonds etc.):

    • o Academic transcripts
    • o English language test results
    • o Graduate entry test results
    • o Three letters of reference
    • o Proof of your professional experience
    • o A copy of your passport

    > Schedule

  • • Advertisement (prospective dates/month): May.

  • • Application procedure :

    • o Go to the official scholarship portal of the Indian government, link is given above
    • o Register and login.
    • o Access the online form and fill out the application
    • o Documents required are attested copies of testimonials, study program details, photographs, etc.

  • • Rounds : Merit based & interview.

  • • How to prepare the best application: N/A

  • • Things to keep in mind: The candidate must not be enrolled in a U.S. degree program or have another degree from any U.S. university and Besides Fulbright-Nehru Master’s Fellowships, the Indian government also partners with the same foundation to provide two other scholarships.

5. The JN Tata Endowment – Higher Education of Indians

  • • Objective : The JN Tata Endowment’s loan scholarship since 1892, when it was established by Jamsetjee Nusserwanjee, the promoter of the Tata group.

  • • Provider : Trust (JN Tata Endowment).

  • • What for : The J. N. Tata Endowment awards a one-time loan scholarship to Indian students for pursuing full-time Postgraduate, Ph.D., Postdoctoral, and Research fellowships abroad.

  • • How many seats : N/A

    > Nature of assistance

  • • Things covered : Scholarship loan ranging between INR 1,00,000 and INR 10,00,000, Partial travel grant of up to INR 50,000, Gift award worth INR 2,00,000 to INR 7,50,000.

  • • Scholarship amount : amount ranges between INR 1,00,000 to INR 10,00,000.

  • • Duration : N/A

  • • Apply Link : jntataendowment.org

    > Qualifications

  • • Ranking requirement : N/A

  • • Academic qualification : The candidate must have a graduate degree from a recognized Indian university with a minimum of 60% marks.

  • • Income criteria : N/A

  • • Residential requirement : yes (The candidate should be an Indian citizen)

  • • Any other (PAN/Bonds etc.): N/A

    > Schedule

  • • Advertisement (prospective dates/month): April.

  • • Application procedure : The candidates have to undergo an online test and interview to be eligible for selection. The online test is not subject-specific; it is based on the Thinking Skills Assessment (TSA). The rigorous technical interview evaluates the candidate's understanding of current and future subjects. The final selection depends on the candidate's test score, interview performance, and academic results scores.

  • • Rounds : Document verification and interview.

  • • How to prepare the best application: N/A

  • • Things to keep in mind: Candidates who could not avail of the loan scholarship previously are also eligible to apply.
