UG Fellowship : Indian

1. Azim Premji Foundation Fellowship

  • • Objective : The Foundation’s vision is to contribute towards a more just, equitable, humane and sustainable society. Its work is focused on India.

  • • Provider : Azim Premji Foundation.

  • • What for : The Foundation offers the unique opportunity to combine professional growth with social commitment and purpose.

  • • How many seats : N/A

    > Nature of assistance

  • • Things covered : tuition fee including Health, Life, and Accident Insurance.

  • • Scholarship amount : INR 35,000 per month and other benefits.

  • • Duration : 01 year.

  • • Apply Link : azimpremjifoundation.org

    > Qualifications

  • • Ranking requirement : N/A

  • • Academic qualification : Must be a graduate degree in any discipline.

  • • Income criteria : N/A

  • • Residential requirement : Yes (Must be an Indian citizen.)

  • • Any other (PAN/Bonds etc.): Proof of Citizenship (birth certificate), degree certificates, etc.

    > Schedule

  • • Advertisement (prospective dates/month): Between August and September.

  • • Application procedure :

      Online through the official website of Azim Premji Foundation

    • • The homepage of the website will open on the screen.
    • • Click on the apply link
    • • Once you will click on the link, an application form will open on the screen
    • • Now, fill in the form with all the required details
    • • After that upload all the required documents
    • • Now, review and recheck the application form to avoid any mistakes
    • • Finally, click on the Submit button to complete the application process

  • • Rounds : Written tests, Physical test and Interview.

  • • How to prepare the best application: N/A

  • • Things to keep in mind: The age of the candidates must be less than 35 years and They must be proficient in English and one of the local languages (Hindi, Kannada, Tamil or Telugu).
